HIGH-LEVEL GOAL
Build a Python prototype that ingests a CSV of pension fund financials, computes input-oriented DEA efficiency (CCR/CRS & BCC/VRS), computes scale efficiency, performs PCA input validation, applies a Simar–Wilson bootstrap bias correction, and runs a RandomForest second stage on bias-corrected scores. Provide a Streamlit UI + a CLI and the ability to export a PDF report.
